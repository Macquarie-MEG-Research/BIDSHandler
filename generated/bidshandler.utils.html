bidshandler.utils.compare_times(time1, conditional, time2)[source]¶

Compares two datetime objects to determine if they are the same.

This differs to normal comparison as it allows for comparison between datetime.date objects and datetime.datetime objects. Equality can thus be determine in such a way that it can be determined if one specfic time on a day is on that day (by equating a date object to a datetime object).
